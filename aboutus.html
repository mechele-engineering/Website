About

Mechelle Engineering is a leading provider of checking fixtures, jigs, and fit-checking solutions for the automotive industry. We specialize in delivering high-quality, precision-engineered components for two-wheeler and four-wheeler plastic and sheet metal parts. With a commitment to quality, durability, and innovation, our solutions ensure perfect fit and accuracy in every component manufactured.

With years of experience in automotive engineering, we specialize in the design, manufacturing, and validation of checking fixtures and jigs. Our skilled team ensures that every component meets stringent industry standards, delivering reliability and efficiency..

Quality is embedded in our process. Every part undergoes rigorous testing and quality control measures to meet industry requirements. From material selection to final fit-check validation, we ensure that each product delivers precision and durability.

At Mechelle Engineering, we integrate cutting-edge technology and advanced prototyping into our production process. With state-of-the-art CNC machining, CMM inspection, and automated quality assurance systems, we maintain high precision in every production cycle.
