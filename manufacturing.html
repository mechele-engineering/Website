Frequently Asked Questions

What types of automobile components do you manufacture?

We specialize in checking fixtures, jigs, gauges, and precision assemblies for two-wheeler and four-wheeler plastic and sheet metal components. Our expertise covers prototype development, production validation, and fit-checking solutions.

Do you offer custom manufacturing services?

Yes, we provide tailored solutions based on specific client needs. Our engineering team works closely with manufacturers to develop customized checking fixtures, jigs, and automotive component validation tools that meet industry regulations.
